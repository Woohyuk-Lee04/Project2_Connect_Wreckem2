Here are a few internships that you may apply to to boost your resume and help with experience for the future.

Lumen Technologies (Data Analyst)


Description: A 10-week program. The candidate is responsible for identifying key sources of improvement for service delivery.

What can you learn: Hands on experience, working with service delivery org, and working with a team.

Requirements: Enrolled at a 4-year accredited college, computer science data majors, data management experience.


Ericsson (Cyber Security)

Description: 12-week program, assisting in multiple projects, help with security-related data and build professional abilities through apprenticeship.

What can you learn: developing and coordinating security, analyze and compile security data, and participate in key security projects.

Requirements: 2.70 GPA, available for full-time for 12 weeks, actively enrolled in an accredited undergrad or masters on a full-time basis.


CACI International Inc. (AI)

Description: A member of a software development team supporting a large, multi-delivery contact supporting national and tactical requirements.

What can you learn: Deep learning and reinforced learning analytics, use critical thinking skills, and assist in organizing and analyzing data for AI/ML projects

Requirements: Interest in AI, machine learning, basic programming knowledge (python), able to work independently and with a team, and strong communication and analytic thinking skills.

  1. What can you gain from landing these internships?
    Landing one of these internships can help boost your resume and may even make the companies want you more or even results in more pay.

  2. How can this help your resume?
    It can boost your resume. It can especially help with the experience part that you may have been missing.
