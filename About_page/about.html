About us

    Our Mission

  • Our mission is to create a simple, accessible, and effective platform where students can discover real project opportunities and companies can easily connect with emerging talent.

    • Who We Are

  • Wreckem 2 is a student team from Texas Tech University dedicated to solving real problems faced by students.

    • What We Built

    NetworkU is a web platform that allows:

  • Students to explore available projects/internships

  • Companies to post opportunities

  • Both sides to connect more effectively

    • Why We Built

    Many TTU students struggle to find local project experience and internships.
    We identified three major pain points:

  • Scattered information across many websites

  • Lack of TTU-specific opportunities

  • Difficulty in contacting companies directly

    • Our Values

  • Simplicity – Easy to use

  • Accessibility – Designed for every TTU student

  • Practicality – Built based on real interviews

  • Team Collaboration – Created through shared development and iterative design
